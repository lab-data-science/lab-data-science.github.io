Data Science Lab

IMT School for Advanced Studies

Instructor: Falco J. Bargagli Stoffi


Overview:

This course will provide an introduction to R for data science and policy evaluation. During each class, a series of applications useful to develop autonomous research projects will be presented. At the end of the course students will be able to perform from scratch a data analysis in R.

Format:

Interactive approach: we will perform simple analyses during the lectures after the main concepts are introduced. Remote lectures are available at this link. The exam will consist in an individual project/presentation using one or multiple tools introduced during the course.

Prerequisites:

R and R Studio should be installed before the first lecture. Here a breif guide on how to install both.
